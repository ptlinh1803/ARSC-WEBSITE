Welcome to ARSC 2022

Al Research Student Conference (ARSC), organised by MLDA@EEE, aims to provide undergraduates and graduates in Singapore with an opportunity to experience an Artificial Intelligence-based research conference to help develop their interest in Al and research.

While gaining technical skills and exposure to AI through our Technical Workshops and Tutorial sessions, the students will also be able to network with industry experts in their respective domain of interest through our Networking Sessions and gain an in-depth insight into some of our industry partners through Company sharing sessions.

Additionally, students would be able to better understand the benefits and requirements of entering the field of AI research and learn more about the available opportunities at our industry partners through the Virtual Job Fair that will be held in Gather.town.

PAPER SUBMISSION: 07/03 - 15/04

Do you have a school-based research paper lying around? Or, do you wish to join an official research conference but don’t know if you meet the conference standards?

If you are looking for a place to showcase your AI idea or project, ARSC paper submission is the thing for you. With our panel of established judges, you can be sure that your research will be evaluated by industry specialists.

We welcome all Al paper submissions with a total prize award worth of 7,500 SGD for the outstanding submissions! The event provides a platform for students to test out your Al research result in the academic field. This will be your chance to submit your FYP/URECA/DIP project paper or post-graduate research paper.

About MLDA@EEE

MLDA@EEE is a lab supporting EEE and IEM students in NTU to learn, adopt skills related to machine learning, data analytics and artificial intelligence.
It is also an AI training and research facility for EEE and IEM students, staff and faculty.
We approach the education of AI in different perspectives, which are 'learn, apply, connect.'
